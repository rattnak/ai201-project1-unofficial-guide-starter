REVIEW: Charlesgate Realty Group — Allston
Rating: 2/5 stars
Reviewer: Former tenant, lived on Glenville Ave 2022-2023

The apartment itself was fine — decent size, good location near the B line. Charlesgate was okay at first but when problems came up they were slow. We had a mouse problem in November that we reported immediately. It took three weeks and four emails before an exterminator came. By that time we'd found evidence of mice in two rooms. The exterminator did come and the problem was resolved but the response time was unacceptable. When we moved out they tried to charge us $400 for "carpet cleaning" even though the carpet was already worn when we moved in. We disputed it and they eventually dropped it but only after we sent them the move-in photos. Always photograph everything at move-in.

REVIEW: RIOS Management — Allston
Rating: 1/5 stars
Reviewer: Former tenant, moved out 2023

RIOS was the worst landlord I've ever had. The heat went out twice during winter. The first time was 10 days with no heat in February — they said the boiler needed a part and there were supply chain delays. We had to buy space heaters. They did not compensate us for the extra electric bill or reduce rent. The second time was 5 days. When we moved out they kept the entire security deposit ($1,400) claiming damage that didn't exist. We had photos of the apartment in perfect condition. We filed with the Massachusetts small claims court and got our deposit back plus court costs but it took 4 months. Do not rent from RIOS.

REVIEW: Boston Realty Advisors — Allston
Rating: 3/5 stars
Reviewer: Former tenant 2021-2022

Boston Realty Advisors managed my building on Commonwealth Ave near BU. They were fine — not great, not terrible. The broker fee was high (one full month's rent) but that's Boston. Maintenance was handled in 1-2 weeks for non-urgent stuff, within 24 hours for emergencies. The main issue was the building was old and the insulation was bad — heating bill averaged $180/month in winter on top of the rent. They were clear about this upfront which I appreciated.
